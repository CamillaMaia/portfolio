How I actually
work with teams.

01

Questions before answers

Reframe the brief in writing. If we can't say what we're solving in one sentence, we're not solving it yet.

02

Research as a habit

Five conversations a month, not five hundred a quarter. Compounding signal beats heroic effort.

03

Prototype the argument

The fastest way to win a debate is to make the disagreement clickable.

04

Ship and revise

Designs aren't finished — they're released. Then we listen again.
